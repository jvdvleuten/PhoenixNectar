# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and constructive.
- Accepting feedback gracefully.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discriminatory language.
- Trolling, insulting, or personal attacks.
- Publishing others' private information.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private report by creating a GitHub issue and labeling it `conduct`.
